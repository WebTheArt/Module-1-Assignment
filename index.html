My Favorite Foods

  • Pizza

    A classic Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It can be customized with ingredients like pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, and more. It is typically baked in an oven, resulting in a delicious combination of flavors and textures.

  • Biryani

    A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and meat (such as chicken, beef, or mutton), or sometimes with vegetables and eggs. It's a flavorful and hearty dish often served on special occasions.

  • Chocolate cake

    A rich and decadent dessert made with layers of moist chocolate sponge cake and covered with chocolate frosting or ganache. Chocolate cake is loved for its intense chocolate flavor and indulgent texture, often enjoyed on special occasions or as a sweet treat.

  • Fuchka (Pani Puri)

    A popular street food snack consisting of hollow, crispy puris filled with spiced mashed potatoes, chickpeas, and tamarind water. It offers a burst of flavors and textures in every bite.
